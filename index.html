Q. Why 82°30'E has been selected as the Standard Meridian of India?
Earth take 4 minute to complete the rotation of 1°. Therefore there is a lag of approximate 30° between eastern most point of india(Arunachal Pardesh) and westernmost point of india(Gujarat). So total time taken to complete the rotation = 4*30 = 120 minute i.e.2hour so to keep the accurate time in the whole country it is taken as the Standard Meridian which passes through Uttar Pardesh in Mirzapur
